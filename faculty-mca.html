Dr. Vijaya Kumar

Principal & Chairman, JNNCE

B.Tech., M.Tech., Ph.D

Dr. Vijaya Kumar brings over 33 years of academic excellence and 5 years of industrial expertise, leading the institution towards innovation, research excellence, and global recognition.

Dr. Y Vijaya Kumar is an accomplished academic leader with extensive experience as Principal across multiple engineering institutions. He served as Professor & Principal at GM Institute of Technology, Davangere (2019-2023) for 3.5 years, and prior to that as Professor & Principal at Sri Sairam College of Engineering, Bengaluru (2013-2019) for over 6 years. He also held the position of Professor & Principal/Dean at JAIN University's School of Engineering and Technology (2009-2013). With a total academic experience of 33 years, a Ph.D. in Operations Research & Statistical Quality Control, and having guided 11 doctoral candidates, he has been recognized with multiple awards including the Best Principal Award in 2017 and has supervised numerous research projects focused on building institutions through academic excellence, research, placement, and innovation.
