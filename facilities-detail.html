About Department & Facilities

About the Department

The Department of Physical Education and Sports was started in the year 1980. JNNCE sports department aims at all-round development of the student's personality through sports activities. Presently, the department has 7 staff members.

It has the required infrastructure to enhance sports activities including in-door sports facilities, out-door sports facilities, and various other amenities. The department actively organizes Inter-departmental sports activities and Annual Sports Day events.

It takes pride in successfully organizing VTU and inter-collegiate sports activities as per the VTU Sports calendar. State-of-the-art facilities enable the department to host selection, training, and practice sessions for college teams, as well as sports events for faculty at college and inter-collegiate levels.

KSCA Recognition

KSCA, Bangalore has recognized and awarded our facility with a trophy and certificate as one of the best turf grounds in Karnataka. The department, along with BCCI and KSCA Bangalore, has conducted:

  • Syed Musthaq Ali South States T-20 Tournament — March 2013
  • India 'A' and West Indies 'A' Four Day Test Match — October 2013
  • Ranji Match between Uttar Pradesh and Karnataka — December 2011
  • BCCI Women's Under-15 One Day Trophy 2022–23 — December 2022
  • BCCI Women's Under-15 One Day Trophy 2023–24 — December 2023

The department has also organized major events such as VTU's 16th Athletic Meet (2013–14), the South Zone Inter University Cricket (M) Tournament 2019, and many more Inter-collegiate, Inter-zone, and Inter-university sports events.

Vision

To run a consistent and sustainable Physical Education and Sports development program providing students with opportunities to participate in recreational and competitive sporting activities.

Mission

Through practice, training, and competition, the department strives to instill in each student:

  • Good sportsmanship and personal integrity.
  • Loyalty to the group and the ability to function with others as a team.
  • Appreciation for hard work, motivation, and perseverance in both winning and losing situations.
  • Pride in achievements made through fair and honest means.

JANVEY - Annual Sports & Cultural Fest

About JANVEY Fest

What is JANVEY?

JANVEY is the premier annual sports and cultural extravaganza of Jawaharlal Nehru National College of Engineering (JNNCE), Shimoga. This highly anticipated 3-day celebration brings together the entire campus community in a vibrant showcase of talent, creativity, and sportsmanship.

JANVEY serves as a dynamic platform for students across all departments—CSE, ISE, ECE, ETE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil, MBA, and MCA—to display their exceptional abilities. The festival is meticulously organized by the college's Cultural Crew and Team EKYAM, fostering campus unity and celebration.

Cultural Competitions

  • Singing: Indian & Western solo/group performances
  • Dancing: Classical, contemporary, and freestyle group performances
  • Pageant: The prestigious Mr. and Ms. JANVEY competition

Fine Arts & Literary

  • Pencil sketching and painting
  • Rangoli and collage-making
  • Photography competitions
  • Quizzes and debates

Sports Events

  • Track & Field: 100m sprint, high jump, shot put, etc.
  • Team Sports: Various inter-departmental competitions

Festival Highlights

JANVEY kicks off with an energetic campus-wide flashmob and features spectacular musical nights showcasing popular Sandalwood playback singers and renowned artists.

Follow us on Instagram @jnnce.janvey for updates!

Training Programs

Aptitude Development Program:

Aptitude development is critical at Graduation and Post Graduation level in today’s environment. Corporates and recruiters evaluate prospective candidates based on their aptitude i.e., quantitative, reasoning, verbal ability, data interpretation and problem solving areas. JNNCE provides training on Aptitude to students in their pre-final year. Trainers are selected based on their experience in teaching students.

Personality Development/Attitude & Behavioural Development Program:

JNNCE emphasizes shaping the personality of each and every student studying at JNNCE to suit their career aspirations. Training programs are conducted for students who wish to pursue higher studies, recruitments, and entrepreneurship areas.

Technical/Domain Skills Training:

Based on the inputs from the advisory Board and Placement Committee, the Training & Placement Cell organizes technical hands-on training in latest technologies to meet the requirements of the industries.

Internship Program

Real-World Industry Exposure

Training & Placement Cell assists students in getting internships in corporate sectors and industries. It provides necessary resources, databases, and takes corporate initiative to conduct internship selection drives in the campus.

Key Benefits of Internships:

  • It helps candidates to understand the practical implementation of the concepts learnt in classroom.
  • Increases the technical knowledge of the students.
  • It increases the chances of getting jobs in the market.
  • Internship helps the students to gain valuable industrial exposure.
  • It increases the overall Employability Skills of the students.

Rules & Regulations

General Conduct

  • Prohibited Substances: Smoking, consumption of alcohol, tobacco, gutka, and other intoxicants are strictly prohibited.
  • Property Care: Students must not cause any damage to hostel furniture, equipment, or property.
  • Explosives: Lighting crackers or using any explosives in or around the hostel is forbidden.
  • Vehicle Policy: Residents are not permitted to keep two-wheelers or four-wheelers in the hostel.

Safety & Security

  • Unauthorized Entry: Bringing outsiders, day scholars, or locals into the hostel is strictly prohibited.
  • Penalty: A fine of Rs. 1000/- will be imposed for bringing unauthorized persons inside.
  • Gatherings: Group gatherings inside or outside the hostel premises are not allowed.
  • Outside Food: Bringing outside food or snacks into the hostel mess/premises is not permitted.

Staff & Management

  • Cleanliness: Mess staff must maintain the highest standards of hygiene and cleanliness.
  • Professionalism: Staff must behave politely with students, parents, and visitors at all times.
  • Property Protection: All staff members are responsible for the protection and maintenance of hostel assets.
  • Duty Integrity: Staff must be punctual and remain dedicated to their duties during working hours.
