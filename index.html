About the College

Jawaharlal Nehru New College of Engineering

Established in 1980, Jawaharlal Nehru New College of Engineering (JNNCE) stands as a premier technical institute in Shivamogga, Karnataka. Affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi, and approved by AICTE and UGC, our institution has been accredited with an "A" Grade by NAAC, reflecting our unwavering commitment to academic excellence.

We offer a diverse range of programs including our flagship BE program, alongside MBA and MCA, fostering a holistic learning environment for over four decades in the heart of the Malnad region.
